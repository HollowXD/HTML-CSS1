My Collection
of Vinyls!

Over the course of a couple of years, I have gone to multiple concerts and grew a love towards music and vinyl's as a whole. I started off small by getting a couple of records and then it turned into multiple. As I was collecting I never had a turn-table, and purely got them to show off what bands I love a specifically which albums I adored.


As you can see on the right, I have collected about 5 different vinyls, some of them containing multiple. I am planning on collecting more down the road but for right now this is what I have.
